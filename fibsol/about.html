Who we are

FIB-SOLLife technologies Pvt. Ltd is a company founded in December 2013, by a team of post doctoral and doctoral scholars of IIT Madras. FIB-SOL currently functions from the Bio-Incubator, IIT Madras Incubation Cell. The company hosts the privilege of being one of the four identified companies to avail the facilities of the newly launched IITM Bioincubator.

To develop an innovative and integrative research approach to offer technical solutions to burning problems in agriculture and healthcare sector. To provide technical empowerment to students and scholars and make the nation self sustained in intellectual segment.
